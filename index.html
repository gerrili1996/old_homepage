Biography

I am a final year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. From Aug 2020 to Mar 2021, I worked with Prof. Justin Solomon as a visiting Ph.D student in the Geometric Data Processing Group at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor degree in Statistics from Chongqing University.

Broadly speaking, my research mainly lies in optimization theory and algorithms, and its applications in machine learning. Recently, I am focusing on the following topics:

  • Optimization over space of probability measures

  • Computational tools for distributionally robust optimization

  • Large-scale computational problems in optimal transport

Feel free to drop me a message if you are interested in my research. (Remote) collaboration is also welcome!
